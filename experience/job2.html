Overview

Led a 4-person senior design team to build an automated component sorting system from concept through working prototype over two semesters.

What I Did

As project lead, I owned the overall system architecture and kept the team on schedule across mechanical, electrical, and software workstreams. I personally handled the PCB design (motor driver + sensor interface board in KiCad) and the mechanical enclosure design in SolidWorks. Coordinated system integration testing in the final month and presented the finished project to a panel of industry judges.

What I Learned

Running a technical project as the lead taught me a lot about scoping, communication, and making decisions under uncertainty. We had a hardware failure two weeks before the final demo — that experience of debugging a PCB under deadline pressure was probably the most valuable thing I took away from the whole year.
