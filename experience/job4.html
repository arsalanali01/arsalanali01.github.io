Overview

Representing Autodesk on campus as a Student Ambassador — promoting design tools like Fusion 360, supporting fellow students in learning CAD, and connecting the engineering community with Autodesk's software and resources.

What I Do

Serve as a peer resource for students learning Autodesk tools including Fusion 360 and AutoCAD, running demos and helping others get up to speed on CAD workflows. Organize and participate in outreach events to raise awareness of Autodesk's student programs, and act as a feedback channel between the student engineering community and Autodesk.

Why It Matters

CAD literacy is foundational for any engineering student, and a lot of people hit the same early walls learning it. Being able to shortcut someone else past those early frustrations — and see them use the tools confidently for their own projects — is genuinely satisfying. It also keeps me sharp and thinking about how to explain technical concepts clearly.
